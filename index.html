FAQ

Do my photos or CSV ever leave my computer?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

Which CSV headers are supported?

first, last (or name), plus optional filename, grade, and teacher. Sorting can be Grade → Teacher → Last → First or any combo of three.

Can I drag to reorder or remove a single image?

Yes. Drag thumbnails to change order and click the hover “×” to remove a single image without re-uploading.

What export sizes are supported?

10×8, 8×10, 15×12, and 20×16 (all at 300 DPI) with exact pixel dimensions suitable for lab and yearbook delivery.
